RackTracker

The essential app for climbers to track gear safety and usage

Track Your Climbing Gear Safety with Confidence

RackTracker helps you monitor the age, usage, and inspection status of all your climbing gear in one place. Never wonder about the safety of your equipment again.

Download on App Store

Key Features

Track Gear Inspections

Record and schedule regular inspections for all your climbing gear with helpful reminders.

Log Climbs

Keep track of how many climbs each piece of gear has been used for to monitor wear and tear.

Manage Racks

Create and customize different gear racks for various climbing styles and locations.

Safety Notifications

Get timely alerts when gear needs inspection or replacement based on manufacturer guidelines.

Quick Filtering

Easily find gear by type, age, status, or inspection date with advanced filtering options.

Safety First

Built with climber safety as the top priority, following industry standards and best practices.
