Keep Your Climbing Gear Safe with Confidence

RackTracker is your comprehensive climbing gear safety companion. Track equipment maintenance, schedule inspections, monitor usage, and get timely retirement reminders based on manufacturer guidelines. Never wonder about your gear's safety again.

Key Features

Gear Inspection Tracking

Schedule and record regular equipment inspections with automated reminders based on manufacturer guidelines.

Climb Usage Logging

Monitor wear and tear by tracking how many climbs each piece of equipment has been used for.

Gear Inventory Management

Organize and customize your climbing gear racks for different climbing styles and locations.

Safety Notifications

Receive timely alerts for gear inspections, replacements, and retirement based on usage and age.

Advanced Gear Filtering

Quickly find equipment by type, age, inspection status, or usage history with powerful filtering options.

Safety-First Design

Built with climber safety as the top priority, following industry standards and manufacturer guidelines.
