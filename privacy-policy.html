Privacy Policy for RackTracker

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to RackTracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Important Information and Who We Are

Purpose of This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy aims to give you information on how we collect and process your data through your use of RackTracker, including any data you may provide through the app.

Controller

Andrew Luden is the controller and responsible for your personal data (referred to as "we", "us" or "our" in this privacy policy).

Contact Details

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@racktracker.app

2. The Data We Collect About You

Personal Data

We collect and process minimal personal data. The app functions primarily with data stored locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.

Data Collected and Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Gear information (names, types, purchase dates, etc.)
  • Rack configurations
  • Climb logs
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and remains on your device unless you choose to back it up via iCloud or other backup methods you control.

Usage Data Collected by Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect usage data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of our app. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App usage data for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or purchasing the ad-free version of the app.

For more information about AdMob's privacy practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple App Store and StoreKit

We use Apple's StoreKit framework to process in-app purchases. Apple may collect:

  • Purchase history
  • Payment information

This information is handled according to Apple's privacy policy, which can be found at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use the data collected primarily to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Display relevant advertisements (in the free version)
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. The data you enter in the app is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@racktracker.app.

6. Third-Party Links

The app may include links to third-party websites, such as manufacturer inspection guidelines. Clicking on those links may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy statements.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@racktracker.app.

By using RackTracker, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this privacy policy.
